Brothers and sisters in Christ,

The words of Paul remind us of the importance of regular gathering within the Church. This is why this group has been organized: in order that we might give more encouragement to one another in the faith, and to serve those who desire greater connection with those of a shared confession of faith. Please prayerfully consider joining us in this growth of the bond of fellowship shared by Lutherans in central Ohio.
