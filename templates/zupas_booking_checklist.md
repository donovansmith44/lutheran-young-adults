# Zupas catering — booking checklist

Reusable for any LYA event that orders Cafe Zupas Build-Your-Own
Buffet (or other Zupas catering). Walk through these in order;
fill the blanks before calling.

---

## Before you call

Have these locked in front of you. The Zupas rep will ask for
most of them on the call.

- [ ] **Event date + service start time:** ____________
- [ ] **Setup-ready time** *(food laid out and hot)*: ____________
  *Typically 15 min before service start.*
- [ ] **Final headcount** *(RSVP-yes count + 2 buffer)*: ____________
- [ ] **Delivery address:** ____________
- [ ] **On-site contact phone** *(probably Donovan's)*: ____________
- [ ] **Dietary brief** *(from RSVP form `dietary_restrictions` answers)*:
  - Vegetarian: ____ count
  - Gluten-free: ____ count
  - Nut allergy: ____ count
  - Other / freeform: ________________
- [ ] **Budget cap** *(for sanity check during the call)*: ____________

## On the call — order details

Read these out / confirm.

- [ ] **Format:** Build-Your-Own Buffet
- [ ] **Headcount confirmed** at [number above]
- [ ] **$12.99/person** confirmed (verify current price — may change)
- [ ] **Delivery fee** quoted: ____________
- [ ] **Tip** *(handled now or at delivery? 18–20% standard)*: ____________
- [ ] **Estimated total:** ____________

## On the call — what's included (verify each)

These are usually included with the buffet but it's not always
explicit. Confirm explicitly so you don't show up day-of and find
out you needed to source plates yourself.

- [ ] Plates *(included? size?)*
- [ ] Napkins
- [ ] Utensils (forks / spoons / knives)
- [ ] Serving utensils for the buffet line
- [ ] Drinks — included? Or BYO?
  - If included: water? lemonade? soft drinks?
  - If not: what's appropriate to provide separately
- [ ] Tablecloths or chafing dishes if hot food

## On the call — service style

- [ ] **Self-serve vs. staff-served?**
  - Self-serve = faster line, works at 25 people, no staff cost
  - Staff service = better portion control, longer line, may add labor fee
- [ ] **How long is food guaranteed hot/fresh?**
  *(Personality Day's dinner slot is 5:15–6:00; food sits out ~45 min)*
- [ ] **Do they stay during service or drop and leave?**

## Confirmation

- [ ] **Get a written confirmation** *(email)*. Forward it to your
      LYA inbox + add a copy to the Trello card as evidence.
- [ ] **Confirmation number / order ID:** ____________
- [ ] **Cancellation window** *(typically 24-48 hr)*: ____________

## Day before

- [ ] **Phone Zupas to reconfirm**: time, headcount, dietary brief
- [ ] Update Trello card with "confirmed" comment + ETA

## Day of

- [ ] **Receive delivery** at the agreed setup-ready time
- [ ] **Inventory** against the order: count items, verify no missing
- [ ] **Sign for delivery** if required
- [ ] **Tip the delivery driver** if not handled in invoice

## Post-event

- [ ] Pay invoice (if not prepaid)
- [ ] Save receipt; attach to budget tracking
- [ ] **Note any issues** (late, missing, low quality) for next time —
      add a comment to the next event's `book_catering` Trello card
