**Subject:** Lutheran Young Adults — summer gatherings to share with your congregation

Dear Pastor [Last Name],

Greetings in Christ! I'm writing on behalf of Lutheran Young Adults, a new group
organized to connect confessional Lutherans across central Ohio — to encourage one
another in the faith and to build genuine fellowship among those of a shared
confession, in the spirit of Hebrews 10:24–25.

We have four gatherings planned through September, and we'd be grateful if you would
share them with the young adults of [Church Name] — from the pulpit, in your bulletin,

  groups, games, a scavenger hunt, and a free catered dinner from Zupas.

- **America's 250th** — Saturday, July 4, 5:00 PM · Bale Kenyon Family Farms
  A laid-back cookout for fellowship, barbecue, and hymn-singing around the bonfire.
  Bring a lawnchair and a snack to share.

- **Clippers Game** — Saturday, August 1, 7:05 PM · Huntington Park (~$30/ticket)
  Cheer on the Columbus Clippers against the Toledo Mud Hens, with a fireworks show
  afterward. We buy tickets as a group, so an early RSVP helps.

- **Community Service** — September 6 · time & location TBD
  We'll open with a devotional, split into groups to serve on projects around Ohio,
  then regather for Vespers, dinner, and fellowship. More details before August.

RSVPs and full details are at https://tally.so/r/Ek8QeA. I'd also be glad to send
