Our Mission
Connection

“And let us consider one another to provoke unto love and to good works: Not forsaking the assembling of ourselves together, as the manner of some is; but exhorting one another: and so much the more, as ye see the day approaching.”

(Hbr. 10:24-25, KJV)

Brothers and sisters in Christ,

The words of Paul remind us of the importance of regular gathering within the Church. This is why this group has been organized: in order that we might give more encouragement to one another in the faith, and to serve those who desire greater connection with those of a shared confession of faith. Please prayerfully consider joining us in this growth of the bond of fellowship shared by Lutherans in central Ohio.

Our First Gathering

Zion at 766 S. High St2:00–6:30 PMPersonality Day
Free Dinner · catered by Zupas

After opening with some hymns and a devotional, we'll all take a personality test and be matched with groups where everyone has tested as having either similar or opposite traits from yourself. This will be an opportunity for us all to get better acquainted, play games, take part in a scavenger hunt, and enjoy a catered meal from Zupas!
